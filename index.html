About Shanice

Welcome! My name is Shanice Smith. I'm a first-generation college student based out of New York City. I'm currently studying Emerging Media Technology at CUNY City Tech, and in this past year, I've been gaining hands-on experience as a Software Engineer intern in both Web Development and Data Science across a number of industries.

As a URM within the field, I aim to diversify the tech industry by bridging connections, sharing resources and encouraging others to pursue tech careers. I’ve been successful in building a network of students, recruiters, industry professionals, and allies to further become a leader for the next generation of technologists. Through Hackathon collabrations and peer-led organizations (join us over at the CUNY Tech discord!), I hope to create a more accessible and inviting tech field.
